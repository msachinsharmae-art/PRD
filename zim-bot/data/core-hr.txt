=== SECTION: EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT ===

Employee Dashboard
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters
The Employee Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of all employees in the organization. Admins can view employee details including name, employee code, designation, department, status, date of joining, and contact information. The dashboard supports advanced filters for entity, department, designation, location, status, and branch. Admins can search employees by name or employee code.

Add Employee Manually
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Take Action > Add Manually
To add a new employee manually, navigate to Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters. Click on the "Take Action" button and select "Add Manually". The system requires entity selection first using a dropdown. Then a multi-step form appears with 7 steps:
Step 1: Personal Details — Name, email, phone, date of birth, gender, blood group, marital status, nationality
Step 2: Employment Details — Employee code, date of joining, entity, department, designation, location, reporting manager, employment type (permanent, contract, intern, consultant)
Step 3: Salary Details — CTC, salary structure, payment mode, bank details
Step 4: Statutory Details — PAN, Aadhaar, PF number, ESI number, UAN
Step 5: Documents — Upload identity proofs, address proofs, educational certificates
Step 6: Previous Employment — Previous employer details, experience, last drawn salary
Step 7: Review & Submit — Review all details and submit
Entity selection is required first before proceeding with the form.

Bulk Employee Upload
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Take Action > Bulk Upload
Admins can bulk upload employees using an Excel template. Download the template from the system, fill in employee details, and upload the file. The system validates the data and shows success or error results.

Employee Profile
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Click on Employee Name
Each employee has a detailed profile page with sections for:
• Personal Information — Name, contact, emergency contacts, family details
• Employment Information — Department, designation, reporting structure, employment type
• Salary Information — CTC breakdown, salary structure, payment history
• Documents — Uploaded documents, ID proofs, certificates
• Assets — Company assets assigned to the employee
• Timeline — Complete history of changes and updates

Employee ID Card
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Select Employee > ID Card
Admins can generate and print employee ID cards with company branding, employee photo, name, designation, employee code, and QR code.

Employee Suspension
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Select Employee > Actions > Suspend
Admins can suspend employees with effective date and reason. During suspension, payroll can be configured to withhold or partially pay salary.

Probation Management
Path: Core HR > Employees > Probation
Track employees on probation period. Configure probation duration per entity or designation. Send confirmation reminders. Extend or confirm probation through the system.

Employee Transfer
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Select Employee > Actions > Transfer
Transfer employees between departments, designations, locations, or entities. Maintain transfer history and effective dates.

Employee Separation
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Select Employee > Actions > Separation
Initiate separation process for resignations, terminations, or retirements. Configure notice period, last working day, and trigger Full & Final settlement process.

Bulk Operations
Path: Core HR > Employees > Employee Masters > Bulk Actions
• Bulk update employee details (department, designation, location changes)
• Bulk status change (active/inactive)
• Bulk document upload
• Bulk salary revision

Organization Chart
Path: Core HR > Employees > Org Chart
Visual representation of the company hierarchy showing reporting relationships. Navigate through departments and teams.

Roles & Permissions
Path: Core HR > Employees > Roles & Permissions
Configure user roles with granular permissions for each module. Default roles include Super Admin, HR Admin, Payroll Admin, and custom roles. Each role can have view, edit, create, and delete permissions for specific modules.

=== SECTION: LEAVE AND ATTENDANCE ===

Shift Management
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Shift Management
Configure employee work shifts with start time, end time, and break duration.
• Fixed Shift — Same timing every day (e.g., 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM)
• Flexible Shift — Variable timings with a window (e.g., login between 8-10 AM, minimum 8 hours)
• Rotational Shift — Different shifts on different days/weeks
• Night Shift — Cross-midnight shifts with special handling

Roster Creation
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Roster
Create and assign rosters to employees for shift scheduling. Support for weekly and monthly rosters. Admins can create rosters manually or use templates.

Attendance Dashboard
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Attendance
Track employee attendance through multiple modes:
• Biometric devices integration
• Web check-in/check-out
• Mobile GPS-based attendance
• IP-based restriction
• Geo-fencing
Configure attendance regularization rules for missed punches.

Leave Rules Configuration
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Leave Rules > Add/Edit Leave Rule
Create and manage leave rules with detailed settings:
• Leave Type — Casual Leave, Sick Leave, Earned Leave, Privilege Leave, Maternity Leave, Paternity Leave, Compensatory Off
• Accrual Settings — Monthly, quarterly, yearly, or on joining
• Carry Forward — Enable/disable with maximum limit
• Encashment — Enable with encashment on (Basic/CTC/Gross) and encashment days
• Clubbing Rules — Restrict combining certain leave types
• Sandwich Rules — Include/exclude weekends and holidays between leave days
• Negative Balance — Allow/restrict negative leave balance
• Probation Leave — Separate rules for probation period
• Half Day Leave — Enable/disable
• Short Leave — Enable with duration settings

Leave Encashment
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Leave Encashment
Configure leave encashment rules per entity:
• Select eligible leave types
• Choose calculation base (Basic, CTC, Gross)
• Set maximum encashable days
• Define encashment cycle (monthly, yearly, on separation)

Holiday Calendar
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Holidays
Configure company holidays:
• National holidays
• Regional/state holidays
• Optional/floating holidays
• Entity-wise holiday calendar
• Upload bulk holidays via Excel template

On-Duty Requests
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > On Duty
Employees can request on-duty or WFH through ESS. Configured approval workflows apply. Track on-duty locations and purposes.

Attendance Regularization
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Regularization
Employees can submit regularization requests for missed punches or incorrect attendance. Admins or managers approve through the configured workflow.

Overtime Management
Path: Core HR > Leave & Attendance > Overtime
Configure OT rules per entity:
• Minimum hours to qualify for OT
• Maximum daily and monthly OT limits
• OT calculation basis (Basic, CTC, Gross, Fixed Amount)
• Weekday, weekend, and holiday OT rates

• Attendance regularization
• On-duty requests

Approval Levels and Hierarchies

• Auto-escalation after configurable time period
• Auto-approval rules for specific scenarios

Email Templates
Path: Core HR > Workflows > Email Templates
Configure email notification templates for:
• Leave application submitted/approved/rejected
• Attendance regularization notifications
• Salary slip generation alerts
• Probation confirmation reminders
• Birthday and work anniversary wishes
• Custom event-based triggers
