Why this exists

Every payroll team I've worked with hits the same wall: rates change, ceilings change, the wage definition itself changes (looking at you, India Code on Wages 2019), and the spreadsheet that ran payroll last quarter is now subtly wrong. Most "global" payroll tools paper over this with hardcoded rates and a release every six months. That's not good enough when ZATCA, GOSI, EPFO, IRS, and CBDT are all updating simultaneously.

The thesis: compliance numbers belong in deterministic rule files, not in code branches and not in an LLM. The AI's job is to narrate what the rule engine computed — never to invent a rate or guess a deadline.

This separation is what makes the tool auditable. Every rate, every ceiling, every deadline ships with the act, section, and effective date. If a rule changes, you edit one file and the dashboard, calculator, and AI explanation all update together.

Who it's for

  • Payroll heads running multi-country teams who need a single source of truth across India, US, UAE, KSA, and Egypt.
  • Finance controllers forecasting employer-side cost of statutory contributions across regimes (Old Acts vs New Labour Codes, Saudi vs expat GOSI, IRS percentage method).
  • HR business analysts validating new payroll vendors against an independent reference engine.
  • Founders and CFOs sizing total compensation cost in a new market before they hire.

What's inside today

  • Payroll dashboard with KPIs, 6-month trend, statutory outflow breakdown, department mix, and employee-level detail per country.
  • 8 calculators: net pay, PF, ESI, gratuity, HRA exemption, TDS, leave encashment, hourly — each with formula and citation.
  • Compliance calendar with payroll-burn analytics overlay — headcount, gross outflow, per-head cost, statutory share.
  • Multi-layer compliance explorer: Federal / State / County / Free-zone drill-down with full statutory coverage.
  • Statutory rule library editable in the UI — every rate, ceiling, deadline persists locally for what-if scenarios.
  • Labour Codes diff tool (India) — Old Acts vs New Codes side-by-side with a live impact calculator on your CTC.
  • AI assistant grounded in the verified knowledge base. Available from any page via the "Ask AI" button.
